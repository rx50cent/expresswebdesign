Common questions

Answers before you even ask

No phone call needed for the basics.

What do I need to get started?

Just your business info, logo (or we can help), and any photos you have. We’ll guide you through everything else.

Do I have to pay monthly forever?

No. Our packages are one-time payments. The only ongoing cost is domain + hosting (~$10–15/month), which you control. Our monthly management plans are optional add-ons.

What if I want changes after launch?

Every package includes 3 days of free edits after launch. After that, small changes are $40/hour or add a monthly management plan.

Can you match my brand colours and logo?

Absolutely. Send us your logo and we’ll match everything. If you don’t have a logo yet, we can help create a simple one.

What if I’m not happy with the design?

We do two rounds of revisions before final payment. We want you to love it — most clients do.

Do you work with restaurants, salons, real estate agents?

Yes — all types of small businesses. Restaurants, clinics, trades, retailers, real estate agents, tutors, and more across the GTA.
